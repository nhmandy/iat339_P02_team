Vera Floral Design Studio

 

Hello!

Vera florist design studio is a unique flower shop inspired to create socially conscious arrangements and living containers. We provide services such as viewing products and placing orders online, delivery as required, customizing floral design for certain occasions. The target audience will be general public. It can range from a child grabbing flowers for Mother's day to preparing flower arrangements for weddings.

Inspiration!

We offer local, urban and ethically traded flowers that cover a wide range of selections. Our company grows plants in a healthy environment that makes flowers unique and appealing. We also offer individually crafted arrangements at an affordable price. The flower shop connects the community and the environment by displaying our beautiful floral design for holiday events and all year round.
