Our Delivery Policy

All of our flowers and gifts are delivered personally in Vancouver and its surrounding suburbs, or by one of our affiliate florists across Canada. At Nature's Wonders Florist, we make every effort to ensure that your order is delivered when desired, and to your specifications. Please be sure to enter all specific requests and delivery information in the "Delivery Notes" field. We will do our best to honour your wishes. For more detailed information other than what is listed below, please contact us at 604-875-7777 or 1-800-606-1338.

Delivery Area

We personally deliver flowers and other gifts from our retail shop to locations in and around Vancouver, including Burnaby, Coquitlam, Delta, Langley, North Vancouver, Port Moody, Port Coquitlam, Richmond, Surrey, West Vancouver and White Rock. In addition, the products we offer on this website are also available for delivery in Canada and the US through our network of affiliated FTD florists.

Same Day Delivery

All of our floral products can be delivered the same day if the order is placed before 1pm in the recipient's time zone, Monday through Friday or before 11am on Saturdays. Orders received after those times will be delivered the following day. Fruit, gourmet baskets and cakes often require 24 hours' notice for delivery.
